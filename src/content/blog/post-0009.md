## Is Kinesis money safe

Kinesis Money is generally considered a legitimate, high-security platform for holding physical gold and silver (/), with assets securely vaulted by third parties like Brinks and Loomis, and verified through regular, independent audits. While it provides secure storage, users should be aware of risks associated with being a non-regulated entity in some jurisdictions, potential withdrawal delays, and general crypto-market volatility. * **Security Infrastructure:** The platform utilizes high-grade security, including biometric authentication and cold storage options.
